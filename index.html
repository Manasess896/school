about us

Spleng School, a premier primary school in Kenya dedicated to providing quality education following the Kenyan curriculum. Our mission is to nurture and develop well-rounded students who excel academically and in co-curricular activities.

Our experienced and dedicated teachers ensure that each student receives personalized attention to help them achieve their full potential.

In addition to our strong academic program, we provide a variety of co-curricular activities to foster the holistic development of our students.

Join us and be part of a vibrant learning community where every child is valued and encouraged to excel.

facilities

classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to foster an engaging and interactive learning environment. Equipped with modern teaching aids, including projectors, smart boards, and high-speed internet, each classroom supports personalized learning experiences. Our dedicated science, computer, and art labs provide students with hands-on opportunities to explore, experiment, and enhance their understanding in key subject areas.

playground

At our school, we believe in the importance of physical fitness and teamwork. Our spacious sports fields are ideal for a variety of outdoor activities, from team sports like football, cricket, and rugby to individual pursuits such as athletics and tennis. We offer students the opportunity to participate in competitive sports, wellness programs, and recreational activities.

transport

Safety and convenience are top priorities for our school transport system. We provide a fleet of well-maintained buses and vans that are equipped with GPS tracking and supervised by trained staff to ensure the safety of students on their daily commute and trips . Our transport service covers a wide radius, making it easy for students to travel to and from school comfortably and punctually.

cafeteria & canteen

Our school canteen and cafeteria offer a wide range of nutritious and delicious meals that cater to diverse tastes and dietary needs. From wholesome snacks to balanced hot meals, we prioritize healthy eating habits for all students. The cafeteria is a vibrant space where students can relax, socialize, and enjoy their meals in a friendly and welcoming environment.

dispensary

Our school dispensary is staffed by qualified medical professionals who provide first aid, health check-ups, and medical care to students and staff. We prioritize the well-being of our school community by promoting preventive health practices and ensuring a safe and hygienic environment.In addition, we recognize the importance of mental health and offer counseling services to support students' emotional and psychological needs. Our trained counselors are available for one-on-one sessions, helping students manage stress, anxiety, and other challenges.
